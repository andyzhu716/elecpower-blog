# Frequently Asked Questions

Informational questions about busbar systems and power equipment.

## General Questions

**Q: What is Isolated Phase Bus (IPB)?**
A: IPB is a metal-enclosed busbar system where each phase conductor is housed in a separate electrically isolated enclosure.

**Q: What is the difference between IPB and Enclosed Busbar?**
A: IPB has each phase in a separate enclosure, while Enclosed Busbar typically has all phases in a shared housing.

**Q: What are the main applications for busbar systems?**
A: Busbar systems are used in power generation facilities, industrial plants, and large commercial buildings for high-current power distribution.

**Q: What factors affect busbar system selection?**
A: Selection depends on application requirements, current ratings, voltage levels, installation environment, and applicable standards.

**Q: What standards may be relevant for busbar system design?**
A: Design may reference international specifications depending on application, region, and regulatory requirements.
