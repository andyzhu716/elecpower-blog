# Applications

Overview of where busbar systems are typically deployed.

## Power Generation

Busbar systems are used to connect major electrical components in power generation facilities.

## Industrial Facilities

Industrial applications include power distribution within manufacturing plants and processing facilities.

## Commercial Buildings

Large-scale commercial installations may use busbar systems for efficient power distribution.
