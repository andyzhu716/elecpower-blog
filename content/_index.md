## What this site is about

ElecPower is a bilingual professional website focused on **busbar solutions for power plants and industrial power distribution**. The primary focus is on:

- Enclosed busbar systems
- Isolated Phase Bus (IPB)
- Common enclosure bus systems

As complementary offerings, the site also covers:

- Transformers
- Energy storage systems
